ProG tutorial mastertorial
=================

**ProG** *(prompt Graph)* is a library built upon :pytorch:`null` `PyTorch <https://pytorch.org>`_ to easily prompt the Graph for Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) structures.

It's based on the paper "all in one: multi task prompting for GNN"(Sun et. al. , 2023)
It consists of various methods from a variety of published papers.

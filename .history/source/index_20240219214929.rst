ProG tutorial
=================

**ProG** *(prompt Graph)* is a library built upon `PyTorch <https://pytorch.org>`_ to easily prompt the Graph for Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) structures.

It's based on the paper "All in one Multi-task Prompting for graph neural networks"(Sun et. al. , 2023)

It consists of various methods from a variety of published papers.
